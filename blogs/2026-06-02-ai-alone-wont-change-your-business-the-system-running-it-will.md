Microsoft executive Jay Parikh argues that enterprise AI success depends on comprehensive infrastructure rather than powerful models alone, with the system running AI being the key differentiator. He outlines Microsoft's integrated agent platform spanning development, contextualization, deployment, governance, and continuous improvement across tools like GitHub, Microsoft IQ, and Foundry. The platform's value compounds over time as agents learn from real-world operations while remaining governed and secure within enterprise systems.
