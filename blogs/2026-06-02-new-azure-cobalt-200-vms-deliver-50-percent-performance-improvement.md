Microsoft announced new Azure Cobalt 200 VMs delivering a 50% performance improvement over previous generations, specifically optimized for modern agentic AI workloads. The Cobalt 200 is Microsoft's custom Arm-based processor designed for cloud-scale AI inference and general compute at enterprise scale.
